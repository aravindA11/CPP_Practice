Exercise 10.7: Determine if there are any errors in the following
 programs and, if so, correct the error(s):
 (a)
 vector<int> vec; list<int> lst; int i;

 while (cin >> i)
     lst.push_back(i);
 copy(lst.cbegin(), lst.cend(), vec.begin());
 (b)
 vector<int> vec;
 vec.reserve(10); // reserve is covered in ยง 9.4 (p. 356)

Ans: 

(a) copy(lst.cbegin(), lst.cend(), back_inserter(vec));
